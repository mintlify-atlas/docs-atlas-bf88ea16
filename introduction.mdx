Welcome to the Android Interview Questions guide — a comprehensive cheat sheet prepared and maintained by **Amit Shekhar**, Founder of [Outcome School](https://outcomeschool.com) and IIT alumnus (2010–14). Amit has taught and mentored hundreds of developers, helping them land high-paying tech roles at product-based companies. This guide distills the most important Android interview topics into focused, interview-ready Q&A.

For video walkthroughs of many of the answers in this guide, check out the [Android Interview Questions and Answers playlist](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Yby0AA7ZSUE) on the Outcome School YouTube channel. Video answers are available for topics including Coroutines, Flow, ViewModel, RecyclerView, and more.
