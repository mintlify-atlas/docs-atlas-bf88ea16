## How to get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Read the introduction">
    Understand the scope of topics and how this guide is organized before diving in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assess your weak areas">
    Work through each section and honestly identify the topics where you need more depth.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Study with intent">
    For each question, don't just read the answer — understand *why* it works that way and be ready to explain it aloud.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Practice explaining">
    Interviews are about communication. Practice explaining answers clearly and concisely, as you would to an interviewer.
  </Step>
</Steps>
